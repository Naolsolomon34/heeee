Help & Writing Guides

College Essay (650 words)

  • Start with a concrete moment (show, don’t tell).
  • Reflect: what changed about you because of this?
  • Use specifics: names, dates, brief numbers (“raised $1.2k”).
  • Trim filler (“very”, “really”, “I think”). Aim for 650–700 words.
  • Close by echoing your opening idea with a sharper insight.

Resume (1 page)

  • Use strong verbs: led, built, launched, shipped, increased.
  • Quantify impact: “Increased signups by 28%”.
  • Keep bullets tight (one line if possible).
  • Group by sections: Education, Experience, Projects, Skills.
  • Use consistent formatting: dates, dashes, punctuation.

Shortcut keys
